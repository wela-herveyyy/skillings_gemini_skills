# Skillings Platform Information

## Core Value Proposition
"A unified academic ecosystem—school-led programs, professional tutors, and structured paths from basics to mastery."

## Key Features
- **Publish Programs:** Manage STEM Bootcamps, College Prep, etc.
- **Scheduling:** Automated batch/schedule management with tutor assignment.
- **Enrollment:** Digital cart, checkout, and enrollment tracking.
- **Tutor Dashboard:** Tutors see their calendar, students, and materials.
- **Payment Flexibility:** Online payments + in-person cashier confirmation.
- **Multi-Campus Support:** Client admins manage several schools from one head office.

## Target Audience
- Private Schools (Preschool to K-12)
- Independent Tutoring Centers
- Educational Freelance Groups
- Test Preparation Centers
